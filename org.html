The Bee Conservancy is dedicated to protecting bees and other pollinators. Their mission is to inspire and engage communities in the conservation of all pollinators, and to work towards protecting their habitats. Their website can be found at: https://www.thebeeconservancy.org/ In addition to their conservation efforts, The Bee Conservancy also offers educational resources and programs to promote awareness about the importance of pollinators and how to support them. They work with communities, schools, and businesses to create pollinator gardens and habitats, and provide guidance on sustainable practices for beekeeping. Overall, The Bee Conservancy is an essential organization that plays a crucial role in the preservation of our natural world.
